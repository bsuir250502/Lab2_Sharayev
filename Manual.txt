Set request -f (first) in command line, to work with results of the fits semestr, and -s (second), to deal with 
the second.

1)Enter full names of students in format(set "end" in field name to stop reading)
  - Name
  - Surname
  - Pathronymic
2)Provide results in format
  - <Exam> <Mark> (1<=Mark<=10)
After this steps information about the students and its results will appear on the screen.
